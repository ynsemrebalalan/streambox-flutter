# IPTV AI Player — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 1, 2026*

## Overview

IPTV AI Player ("the App") is a media player application developed by Yunus Emre Balalan. This privacy policy explains how we handle information.

## Data We Do Not Collect

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Name, email address, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports sent to our servers

## Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **only on your device**:

- Playlist URLs and Xtream Codes credentials you enter
- App settings and preferences
- Recently watched channels
- Favorites list

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## Third-Party Services

The App connects only to URLs and servers **you provide**. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party services you choose to connect to.

## AI Features

If you use AI summary features, your program title may be sent to an AI API provider (OpenAI or Groq) that you configure with your own API key. We do not store or process this data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
